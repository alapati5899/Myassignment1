My HomeTown

  1. Visakhapatnam also known as “Vizag” is the administrative headquarters of Visakhapatnam district in Andhra Pradesh. Vizag also known as the “City of Destiny” or termed by many as the “Goa of East Coast” in India is one of the oldest and richest in terms of its being, culture and heritage. Renowned for its tourist spots, industrial growth, Sea trade, Vizag is considered to be the commercial capital of coastal Andhra Pradesh. This “Jewel of East Coast” is the headquarters of Eastern Naval Command.
